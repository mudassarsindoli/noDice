What is noDice !

Tired of waiting for your turn to roll the dice... I present to you the noDice!

noDice is a simple and cool website which is designed specifically for playing Board games. Basically... its a online dice which which will generate a new number everytime you hit the "Roll Dice!" button. With this you can play any board games like Ludo with your friends at home or office or anywhere else.

This website works well on Desktop/Tablets/Mobile screens. It is optimized for all the platforms.

And also this website can be used by anyone. This website does not contain any explicit content and does not promote anything. It is just a small project designed and developed by Me.
